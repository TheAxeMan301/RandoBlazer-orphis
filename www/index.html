Soul Blazer Randomizer

RandoBlazer is a randomizer for the classic SNES Super Nintendo action adventure RPG game Soul Blazer. The equipment, magic and items you need will be scattered from Grass Valley to the World of Evil. Free the Freilian souls, collect the stones, dance with the grandmas and defeat Deathtoll in a journey that changes with every seed.
